About Me

Antonio Simonetti comes from an old neapolitan family of glassmakers and mirrors silverer, and has continued the tradition always taking care of glass work, with in-depth studies at the Academy of Fine Arts in Naples. He acquired a mastery in the art of painting in focus unique in the sector.

Lives and works in Naples, where he has the artistic laboratory ARTIC49 arti&cult, located in a central street of the city.

Follow the tradition of medieval painting technique, and is now one of the few to know the secret of painting a large fire; his works are imported all over the world and also boasts an execution in the Vatican City.

Specializing in the restoration technique of ancient stained glass windows.
